## The motivation

Every developer needs a space to document what they learn. This blog is that space — a **read-only portfolio project** that proves I can wire up routing, parse markdown, and ship a polished UI without a backend.

## What I wanted to practice

- **React** component composition and reusable layouts
- **React Router** for clean, shareable URLs
- **Vite** for fast local development and static builds
- **Markdown + frontmatter** as a lightweight content layer

## What comes next

This MVP focuses on reading posts. Future iterations might add search, a dark theme, or a headless CMS — but for now, the goal is a solid foundation that looks good in a portfolio.

> Good portfolios show not just what you built, but how you think about structure and user experience.
